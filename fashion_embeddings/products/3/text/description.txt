These rugged, multifunctional shorts have a mesh liner and are designed for use in and out of the water. They're built of quick-drying NetPlus® 100% postconsumer recycled nylon faille made from recycled fishing nets to help reduce ocean plastic pollution; with a durable water repellent (DWR) finish made without intentionally added PFAS. Inseam is 7". Made in a Fair Trade Certified™ factory.
